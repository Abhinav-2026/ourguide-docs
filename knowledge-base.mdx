The Knowledge Base is where your Ourguide agent learns about your product. Add sources so the agent can answer user questions accurately.

## Source Types

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Website" icon="globe">
    Crawl and index your website pages
  </Card>
  <Card title="Files" icon="file-lines">
    Upload PDFs, docs, or text files
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notion" icon="book-open">
    Connect your Notion workspace
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Adding a Website Source

1. Go to **Sources > Website** in your dashboard
2. Enter your website URL
3. Click **Crawl** — Ourguide will index all linked pages
4. Review the indexed pages and remove any you don't want

## Uploading Files

1. Go to **Sources > Files** in your dashboard
2. Drag and drop files or click to upload
3. Supported formats: PDF, TXT, DOCX, MD

## Connecting Notion

1. Go to **Sources > Notion** in your dashboard
2. Click **Connect Notion**
3. Authorize access to your workspace
4. Select the pages you want to index

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  Keep your knowledge base focused on what users actually ask about. Quality matters more than quantity.
</Tip>

- **Be specific** — Add detailed product docs, not just marketing pages
- **Stay current** — Re-crawl your website when content changes
- **Test regularly** — Ask the agent questions to verify accuracy
